Reservation Policies

⏰
Reservation Hold Time

We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the booking time. If you're running late, please call us at (619) 501-5505 so we can accommodate you.

👥
Large Parties (13+)

For groups of 13 or more, please use our private events inquiry form or call us directly. Large parties require advance coordination with our events team.

🚶
Walk-Ins Welcome

Walk-ins are always welcome, subject to availability. Bar seating and our lounge areas often have space, especially earlier in the evening.

🎂
Special Occasions

Celebrating a birthday or anniversary? Let us know in your reservation notes and our team will do our best to make it extra special.

📞
Modifications & Cancellations

Need to change or cancel your reservation? Modify directly through OpenTable or call us at (619) 501-5505 as early as possible.
